Introduction

what is IntelligentAdvertiser?

IAdvertiser is an advertisement recommender concept developed to recomend television commercials for the user.

We address a common problem most user's face when watching tv which is displaying irrelavant advertisements.The advertiser is developed to gather user data while infron of the tv to recommend the most suitable advertisements for them.It is also well secured using ethereum blockchain to encrypt data and information.
